About WOAgriTech

Founded in Nairobi by agricultural trade experts, we bridge the gap between Kenya's farming communities and the European Union's market requirements.

Our Story

Born from Kenya's Export Crisis

In 2021, Kenya lost over KES 4,000 billion in export revenue due to rejected shipments at EU ports. Products failed to meet pesticide residue limits, food safety standards, and increasingly, EU environmental requirements. Thousands of smallholder farmers — who had invested entire seasons into their crops — watched their livelihoods disappear.

WOAgriTech was founded in 2022 by a group of agricultural trade lawyers, ex-Kenya Bureau of Standards officers, and EU policy specialists with a single mission: ensure no Kenyan farmer loses income due to lack of regulatory knowledge.

Today, we have supported over 500 farmers and exporters across 18 Kenyan counties, providing EUDR compliance support, food safety audits, training, and EU buyer connections.

Our Journey

2021

Kenya loses KES 4,000B in EU rejected exports. Founders identify critical regulatory knowledge gap.

2022

WOAgriTech founded in Nairobi. First pilot program with 50 coffee farmers in Kiambu County.

2023

EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR) enters into force. WOAgriTech becomes Kenya's first dedicated EUDR consultation firm.

2024

Expanded to 18 counties. Launched farmer training in Swahili. 500+ farmers served. 98% compliance rate.

2025 →

Pan-Africa expansion. Partnership with EAC to support Tanzania, Uganda, and Rwanda farmers.

What Drives Us

Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

A Kenya where every farmer — regardless of education level or location — has equal access to lucrative EU markets through regulatory knowledge and compliance support.

Our Mission

To eliminate regulatory barriers for Kenyan agricultural exporters by providing expert EUDR compliance consultation, practical training, and supply chain documentation support.

Our Values

Farmer First — we serve smallholders, not corporations. Transparency — honest assessment of compliance risks. Impact — measurable export income improvement.
